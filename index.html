Welcome to Leaders in Engineering & Computer Science Council (LECS) at CSUN

Explore opportunities to grow, connect, and lead within the engineering community. Whether you're interested in joining the council, attending events, or simply looking for resources to help you along the way, we're here to help you make the most of your time at CSUN.

How LECS Helps You Succeed

Become Part of Our Community

At LECS, we're all about connection. Whether you're new to CSUN or have been here a while, we're here to help you find your place in the engineering and computer science community.

Get Involved in Events

We host a variety of events throughout the year, from hands-on workshops to casual meetups. It's all about learning together, sharing ideas, and building friendships that can last well beyond your time at CSUN.

Tap into Our Resources

We know the journey through engineering and computer science can be challenging, but you don't have to do it alone. We offer resources like study groups, career advice, and more—everything you need to navigate your academic and professional path.

Take on Leadership Roles

If you're looking to make an impact, there are plenty of opportunities to lead within LECS. You can help organize events, mentor peers, or start new initiatives—whatever feels right for you.
